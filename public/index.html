Who is Helix for?

Two doors into the same system. One for people who want the work done, one for people who build.

For operators

Non-technical people who just want to utilise AI. Hire an agent or a team, tell it what you need, in your own words or your own voice, and stay safe while you do it. No dependence on any single large lab, and no infrastructure to run.

For builders

Developers who want to reuse the platform's capability inside their own agentic workloads:

  • Cortex memory, sovereign and self-hosted
  • Skills created in your workflows, reusable everywhere
  • A rules engine that makes agents deterministic where it matters
  • Generative UI: the interface builds itself around the task
  • Voice as a first-class interface across all of it

Privacy notice

A company selling sovereignty does not trade your details. Short, in plain words.

What we collect. Name, work email, company (optional), which products interest you, and anything you write about your use case. Signing up through the chat collects the same details, nothing more.

Why. To email you about early access to the products you chose. That is the whole purpose. The lawful basis is your consent, given by the checkbox on the form.

Where it lives. On infrastructure MindLynx controls. It is never sold, never shared with third parties, and never used to train anyone's model.

How long. Until the products reach general release, or 12 months after we last hear from you, whichever comes sooner. Then it is deleted.

Your rights. Email hello@helix.work with "remove me" and your record is deleted. You can ask what we hold about you the same way. No cookies are set on this page.

Voice. The voice conversation is optional and streams audio to our speech provider only while the microphone is on. The page works fully by text.
